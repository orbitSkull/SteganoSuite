How It Works

Invisible Characters

Uses zero-width space (U+200B) and zero-width non-joiner (U+200C) Unicode characters that are completely invisible.

Undetectable

The stego text looks identical to normal text. Only specialized tools can detect the hidden data.

Copy-Paste Safe

Hidden characters are preserved when copying and pasting across most applications and platforms.
